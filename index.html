background

04 case study

Visual Design Curriculum

Extensive data visualization training courses and workshops, developed and implemented across a Fortune 500 company.

open case study
header

Exploring Resource Description Framework (RDF) data models can be very cumbersome, particularly when they require complex queries to retrieve information. Also, they typically visualize results in poorly contextualized tables. The graph explorer tool helps simplify the process by binding queries to a more intuitive network graph with point and click style navigation.
